Reservation Policies

Late Arrival

We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time. After that, tables may be released for walk-in guests.

Cancellation

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance for a full refund of the reservation fee.

Group Size

For parties larger than 10, please contact us directly at (123) 456-7890 for special arrangements.

Payment

The reservation fee is $10 per person and will be applied as credit to your final bill.
